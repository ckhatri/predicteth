Predicteth


Hello, if you'd like to utilize the smart contracts for peer to peer betting go here: for the smart contracts: https://github.com/ckhatri/predicteth/tree/master/contracts

You can download these files to your local computer (You do not need the migrations file).

next go to https://remix.ethereum.org and then upload all of those files. Compile. Deploy to whichever network you want to, test or live.

For the time stamp you can use: https://www.epochconverter.com/

Bet price is the price you believe ethereum will be above at the deadline.

Bet amount is the amount you want to bet, note that this number is / 1000 ether so if you want to be 1 ether you'd write 1000 here (this has to do with solidity not doing decimals or floating point). So if you enter 1 you'd be betting 0.001 ether.

Have fun!
